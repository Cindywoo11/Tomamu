Why Tomamu?

You may ask why Tomamu? Simply because there are so much to explore in this delighful spot!

Tomamu is quickly becoming recognized as a world class ski resort with perfect snow quality. Due to the region low atmosepheric temperature, the snow condition is amongst the best in the prefecture,and with clear beautiful skies making up more than 75% of the season, anyone can totally enjoy powdery slopes in optimal conditions.

There are many fun and interesting activties other than skiing catering to both adults and children, such as the Ice Village, where you can experience a world of ice at -30 degrees, and the Terrace of Frost Trees, where you can enjoy the views of a white snow-covered world reaching as far as the eyes can see.

Tomamu is also renowned for its fresh seafood and premium Wagyu beef and its award winning locally brewed beer, all ready to tantalise your taste buds. And what's more satisfying than indulging yourself in Japan's largest onsen after a ski day!
